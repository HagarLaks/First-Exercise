My favourite movie these days:

Encanto

Encanto on IMDB

Encanto is a very inspiring movie. It seems like a kids movie but it is an amazing movie for adults. The main charachter is Mirable, a Colombian teenage girl has to face the frustration of being the only member of her family without magical powers. You'll meet her family members, find out their special powers and I am sure you will find at least one charachter to identify with.

This is Luisa, Mirable's sister. Her power is strenght. She lifts, carries, and never breaks.

Quoting her: "Why Would Anything Be Wrong? I'm Totally Fine. The Magic's Fine. Luisa's Fine. I'm Totally Not Nervous."
